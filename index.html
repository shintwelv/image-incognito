How It Works

Three steps to private.

1

Pick a Photo

Select any photo from your library or take one with your camera. Incognify handles the rest.

2

Faces Detected

The app automatically finds every face using Apple's Vision framework — no tapping, no cropping required.

3

Choose Your Mask

Pick your aesthetic: blur, pixelation, or more. Adjust individual faces or apply a style to all at once.

4

Share Freely

Export in full resolution and share directly to Instagram, Messages, or save back to your Photos library.
